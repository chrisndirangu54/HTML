About Us

Building Kenya’s Future with Data-Driven Apps

TeknTandao, based in Nairobi, is on a mission to transform Kenyan businesses with modern, intelligent, and seamless applications. Our partnership with American Corner Mtaani under the US Embassy underscores our commitment to impact. Apps like Bima Guardian (InsureTech) and Sokonis (FoodTech) deliver responsive, data-driven solutions for Africa’s challenges.
